ARLINGTON COTTAGES & TOWNHOMES

Design and Project Manager

Capstone Collegiate Communities contracted with Capstone Interiors in 2016 to provide student residential unit furniture, outdoor pool furniture, and a unit model for a new construction off-campus housing project in Baton Rouge, Louisiana near Louisiana State University. The project included a $900k student residential unit budget and 400 beds of furnished student units, $275k in outdoor furniture, and a 4-bedroom townhouse leasing model that was furnished and accessorized to appeal to student tenants.
