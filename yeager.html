Yeager Residence

Designer and Project Manager

The Yeager family enlisted Laura Harris to lead the renovation of their 1920s Birmingham bungalow in the Crestwood neighborhood. After 12 years in the home, the family of four needed to reconfigure the single-bathroom residence to accommodate their evolving needs while preserving the character of the historic house.

Laura drafted construction plans to maximize the existing footprint with only a 3-foot-deep addition across the rear of the home. The project scope included adding a second bathroom, enlarging closets, and closing off impractical doorways. She brought the basement staircase up to code to create a finished living area with a Murphy bed and reconstructed the utility room into a primary bath, walk-in closet, and multi-purpose laundry room. To maintain budget, Laura prioritized reusing existing doors, windows, and plumbing locations wherever possible. The project was featured in Southern Living.
