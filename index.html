Laura Harris is a registered interior designer with over nine years of commercial interior design experience. She graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interior Design from Samford University, which offers an undergraduate interior design program accredited by the Council for Interior Design Accreditation (CIDA). In 2009, she passed the Leadership in Energy and Environment Design (LEED) exam. She also passed the National Council for Interior Design Qualification (NCIDQ) examination in 2012 and became a registered interior designer in the state of Alabama. She is a volunteer on the board of the International Interior Design Association (IIDA) Alabama Chapter, which coordinates professional events for the interior design community including joint ventures with the American Society of Interior Designers (ASID) and the Alabama Interior Design Coalition (AIDC). She expects to receive a Master of Fine Arts in Interior Design degree from Brenau University in May 2019.
